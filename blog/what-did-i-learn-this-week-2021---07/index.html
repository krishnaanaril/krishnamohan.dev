  1. Author of famous NewtonSoft.json is a kiwi(?) named James Newton-King

  3. Chimerism: Recently I read about the case of a failed dna test in time magazine. Chimeras are animals or humans that contain the cells of two or more individuals. Their bodies contain two different sets of DNA.
